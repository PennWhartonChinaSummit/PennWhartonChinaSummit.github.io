InnoTalk is an annual startup pitch competition held by Penn Wharton China Summit. We are committed to providing a platform for young creative entrepreneurs who have started their ventures with ambitions to impact different communities. This year we will be holding our third InnoTalk at the Wharton School on April 11th, 2020. Participating teams will have the opportunity to receive one-on-one guidance from famous startup CEOs. The winning teams will be awarded with exclusive mentoring sessions with investment partners at prestigious venture capitals (e.g. Zhen Fund) in addition to prize money. By rewarding hard-working entrepreneurs and paving the way for innovation in future business world, InnoTalk is dedicated to promoting entrepreneurship within international student communities among different institutions in China and the United States.
